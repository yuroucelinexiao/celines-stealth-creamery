P1.1 Monkey Choconutty

Recipe

Ingredients for a 24-oz pint:

  • Whey protein, chocolate (1 scoop / 35 g)
  • Peanut butter powder (2 tbsp)
  • Cocoa powder (1 tbsp)
  • Salt (pinch)
  • Banana (1 large / 134 g)
  • Milk, 0% fat, ultra-filtered (2 cups)
  • Brown sugar, light (1 tbsp)
  • Mixed nuts, crushed (50g)

Instructions:

  1. Blend everything except nuts together
  2. Freeze for 24 hours
  3. Spin on LITE ICE CREAM (push down and re-spin if needed)
  4. Add in nuts and spin on MIX-IN

Reflections

Overall Rating: ★★★★☆

What is more satisfying than hitting your protein goals in the form of ice cream? This recipe is one of my favourite flavour combinations: chocolate, peanut butter, and banana. Despite a strong protein powder taste, this pint was very delicious.

The Good (what went well):

  • The final texture was creamy: Despite what the instructions say, if the first spin is too powdery, then re-spinning before mix-ins help to create a smoother texture.
  • The nuts were evenly incorporated: Make a well for the mix-ins.

The Bad (lessons learned):

  • Protein powder aftertaste: Add in stronger flavours (e.g. sweetner, cocoa, peanut butter) to mask the taste.
  • The powders stuck to the bottom of the pint: Pour in some liquids before powder.
